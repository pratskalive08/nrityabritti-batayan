Nrityabritti Batayan

The Guru

Kalamandalam Swarnadeepa Mahanta, an Indian classical dancer is a graded artist of the broadcasting media-Dooradarshan Kendra Kolkata. She has performed not only in several prestigious Indian dance festivals, but has also been invited abroad to countries like Germany, Netherlands & Sweden among others to perform and conduct workshops. She takes a keen interest to spread the joy of Indian Classical dance and to inspire the youth towards it. She coaches aspiring dancers in the form Mohiniyattam, Kuchipudi and Bharatanatyam.
